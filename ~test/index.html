Bio

Dr. Marcus J. Reinhardt is an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science at the Central Institute of Technology. His research lies at the intersection of distributed systems, cloud-native infrastructure, and edge computing. He focuses on designing scalable, resilient platforms for large-scale data processing in heterogeneous environments.

Dr. Reinhardt has led multiple industry–academia collaborations on deploying distributed systems in production environments, particularly in smart cities and real-time analytics. His work has been published in top-tier venues and has influenced modern microservice and edge orchestration frameworks.

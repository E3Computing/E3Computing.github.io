Bio

Dr. Marcus J. Reinhardt is an Associate Professor specializing in distributed systems and cloud-native infrastructure. His research focuses on building scalable and resilient platforms for large-scale data processing, with particular interests in edge computing and system reliability. He actively collaborates with industry partners on the real-world deployment of distributed architectures.
